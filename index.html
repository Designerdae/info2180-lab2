How did I go from Business to Computing?

October 2, 2021 by Dana Edwards

Hi! I'm Dana also known as "D" or "Dae" and I fell for computing. I attended the Convent of Mercy Academy 'Alpha,'and as early as first form, we were introduced to Information technology. I took an interest in this since then and continued to pursue it. However, as I progressed through high school, I also developed a liking for business the more I explored, minus the accounts part. "Ensure you get the balance," it's a no from me, nonetheless I passed. It was around fourth form when it was time to answer the question, what do you want to do? My school grouped a set number of subjects together for each class and everyone from that class would do the same subjects. Funny enough, I chose a science class, but my coordinator at the time said to me that she feels I would do well in the business class, which also had I.T. At first, I was put off like what? you could do that? but, I took her advice and I must say, I dont regret it.

Throughout liking business, my interest for Information technology grew, I was enjoying it so much that at one point, I didnt even have to study for tests. SBAs were stressful but I enjoyed it nonetheless, I actually scored full marks on my I.T. IA! Ofcourse, when it was time to apply for university, it was a no brainer, Information technology was my first choice and Entrepreneurship as my second. Very quick, I got accepted to Entrepreneurship and my reaction? I thought cool, going to UWI but the excitement wasn't at its peak. A couple months later, coincidentally, I'm in I.T. class and I stumbled upon an email from UWI. I got accepted to the I.T. program! I was elated, I almost screamed in class :D. That is when I thought to myself, let the journey begin, I don't know where in tech I will go, but I want to be there.

You made it, What's next?

October 2, 2021 by Dana Edwards

Just as many freshers at the begnning of university, I was beaming with excitement, ready to start my journey. Sparkles in my eyes if you will, and then the workload came and said hey are you ready for me? Maybe I wasn't so ready. I orginally came to pursue Information technology but after making comparisons, asking questions and so forth, I switched to a Computer Science major. I knew tech was where I wanted to be, but the extensive math was a struggle.

I thought, is that all there is to Computing? there must be something that would suit me here. I began to research and I discovered UX/UI Design. Bingo! this is something that intrigued me. Plus, I already liked to dabble in a bit of design with posters and logos, seeing what colour combinations worked well, and creating 'pleasing to the eye'designs. I try to manage my time wisely, but sometimes it doesn't work out so well. However, this summer, I took an introductory course on User Experience online, to grasp what it entailed and if it was for me. I enjoyed it and decided this is something I see myself doing. As it relates to Web Development, I got a small taste of it, in high school and I found it really cool, got excited over changing the colour of a font and so on. But, the interest grew more recently,from doing Web Development labs. It didn't feel like a drag to do the lab, it was an oportunity to know more and have fun. As such, my journey to becoming a UX/UI Designer and Web Developer is looking promising.
